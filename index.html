About

As Head of the Prospective Risk Analysis Department at the Central Bank of Chile, I have over 15 years of experience in leading and conducting macroeconomic and macrofinancial risk assessments for the Chilean economy. I apply my skills in applied methods to analyze and research topics such as credit markets, household finance, macroprudential policy, mortgage markets, climate change and financial stability in general.

I also contribute with policy insights to the Financial Stability Report and Financial Policy Meetings, where the Bank's board discusses and decides on the macroprudential policy stance. I coordinate the research agenda of my department and collaborate with other areas and external partners on projects and initiatives that support the Bank's mission and vision. I value teamwork, innovation, and excellence, and I strive to bring diverse perspectives and experiences to my work.
